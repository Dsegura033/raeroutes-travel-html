RaeRoutes Holiday Travel Gift Experience

The most meaningful gift isn’t a thing, it’s the trip you’ll remember forever. Give the gift of going this holiday season!

How It Works

1. The Gifter Fills Out the Gift Request

You (the gifter) decide the budget range, trip style, and what you want included. You’ll fill out the holiday gift request form so RaeRoutes can prepare custom options.

Fill Out Gift Form

2. You Approve the Trip Plan & Pay

I break down room options, package pricing, flights (if needed), and any add-ons. Everything must be booked under the receiver’s name using the gifter’s card through the RaeRoutes authorization form.

3. The Receiver Gets a Beautiful Trip Reveal

Once everything is booked, the receiver will get a customized trip reveal page with all the details of their upcoming getaway, destination, hotel, dates, and any add-ons you included.
